FAQ

Your Questions,
Answered.

Everything you need to know about how FinanceSME works and what to expect from your application.

What types of businesses can apply?
▾
FinanceSME works with all types of South African businesses — from start-ups seeking launch capital to established SMEs looking for expansion or working capital. Whether you are a sole proprietor, close corporation, or registered company, you are welcome to apply.
Is there a cost to submit an application?
▾
There is no upfront cost to submit your application through FinanceSME. We work on a success-based model, meaning our fee is only applicable once a funding offer is accepted by you.
How long does the process take?
▾
Once your application is submitted, we commit to providing you with feedback within 2 business days. The full process from application to final approval depends on the lender, but our intermediary role is designed to accelerate every step.
Will applying affect my credit score?
▾
Your initial enquiry with FinanceSME does not trigger a credit bureau inquiry. A formal credit check is only conducted once a lender is ready to proceed with a formal offer, which you will be informed of in advance.
What documents will I need?
▾
Requirements vary by lender and loan type, but typically include: recent bank statements (3–6 months), management accounts or financial statements, business registration documents, and ID documents for directors. We will guide you through exactly what is needed after your initial enquiry.
What is the minimum and maximum loan amount?
▾
We assist with funding from under R 500,000 all the way through to R 20 million and above, depending on your business profile and the lenders best suited to your needs.
